# StudyLedger — Brand Profile & Launch Script

> Compiled 2026-07-03 from founder questionnaire (28 questions, 7 rounds).
> Governs the launch video and all downstream assets (social kit, teasers, landing alignment).
> Language: English only.

---

## 1. Brand Core

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Launch subject | StudyLedger as a whole — full platform + brand introduction |
| Category framing | **"Your study ledger"** — lean into the name; a permanent record of effort |
| Core promise | **Accountability that works** — a system that makes quitting harder than continuing |
| Positioned against | Single-purpose edtech apps (Quizlet, Anki, Forest…) that don't connect |
| Origin story | Built by a student + "Money has ledgers. Businesses have ledgers. Why doesn't your education?" |
| Market frame | Global-first — world-class SaaS aesthetic, no regional markers |
| End feeling | "I need this now" + "This looks premium" |
| Never | Preachy · Corporate · Overwhelming |

## 2. Audience

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Primary viewer | All students — don't narrow |
| Emotional entry states | Guilty scroller · Burnt out · Ambitious optimizer · Lost — hook must work for all four |
| First touch | Instagram Reels (9:16, sound-on, 2s hook) + Twitter/X & LinkedIn |
| Key belief to land | **"I won't quit this time."** |

## 3. Voice & Tone

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Persona | **The quiet professional** — minimal words, confident, Apple energy |
| Humor | One wink only — placed mid-video during the 48-tool sweep |
| Sentence style | Question-led — "What if you could see every hour you've ever studied?" |
| Delivery | Voiceover-driven; visuals support the narration |
| VO voice (decided from script) | Calm low-mid male, unhurried, intimate — quiet authority, NOT movie-trailer bombast |

## 4. Visual Identity

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Visual world | Hybrid: opens in real messy student life → transitions into clean product universe |
| Palette arc | **Dark → light** — starts dark (the struggle), ends light (the clarity) |
| Base | Near-black `#08090c` / `#0f1016` → resolving to ink-light `#f0f0f5` |
| Hero accent | Coral/cinnabar `#ff7c5c` (with `#ff6b6b`) — the brand-moment color |
| Secondary | Indigo `#818cf8` · category colors: lime `#a3e635`, cyan `#22d3ee`, orange `#fb923c` |
| Product shots | Hero UI moments — a few beautiful slowed-down shots (streak filling, score rising) |

## 5. Launch Video Spec

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Length | 30 seconds (hero cut) |
| Format | 9:16 vertical primary (Reels), 16:9 secondary |
| Hook | The relatable fail — abandoned plans, dead streaks |
| Mid-video spotlight | The 48-tool sweep |
| Close | CTA — "Start your ledger — studyledger.in" |
| Music | Minimal → swell: sparse piano/pulse under the struggle, cinematic swell at the turn, clean resolve at CTA |

## 6. Roadmap After Hero Video

1. Hero 30s launch film (this script)
2. Teaser sequence — 2–3 short cryptic teasers pre-launch
3. Social asset kit — stills, thumbnails, PFP, banners (IG/X/LinkedIn)
4. Landing page alignment — studyledger.in hero copy matches the film's language

## 7. The Script — "Started Over" (30s hero cut)

**Palette arc:** `#08090c` black → coral `#ff7c5c` ignition → `#f0f0f5` light.
**Music:** solo piano pulse → strings swell at 0:11 → clean resolve at 0:27.
**VO:** calm low-mid male, intimate, unhurried.

| Time | Visual | VO / Text | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:03 | DARK. Real life, cinematic: a phone alarm swiped away. A paper planner open to Day 3 — blank after that. A habit app resetting to "Day 1." | VO: **"How many times have you started over?"** | Sparse piano pulse. Room tone. |
| 0:03–0:08 | Faster cuts: 14 browser tabs. Six study-app icons on a home screen. Flashcards untouched, badge count "247". A YouTube "how to study" video paused at 0:44. | VO: **"New app. New plan. Gone by week two."** | Pulse tightens. A soft clock tick enters. |
| 0:08–0:11 | THE TURN. Screen goes to pure black `#08090c`. Beat of silence. A single coral line `#ff7c5c` draws itself across the dark — like a ledger rule being inked. | VO: **"Money gets a ledger. Businesses get ledgers. Why doesn't your effort?"** | Everything drops out. One piano note. Strings begin to swell. |
| 0:11–0:20 | THE SWEEP. The coral line unfolds into UI. 48 tool cards cascade past in an accelerating rhythm — exam simulator, focus timer, study rooms, flashcards — each catching light. Palette lightens with every beat. **THE WINK (0:17):** the sweep stalls a half-beat on one card, then VO, dry: | VO: **"What if it was all in one place — every hour, on record? Forty-eight tools."** *(beat)* **"You'll meet most of them the night before an exam."** | Swell building. Each card lands on a beat. |
| 0:20–0:26 | BELIEF. Light mode now, `#f0f0f5`. Two hero UI moments, slowed down: a Study Room pact holding — four avatars, nobody bails. A score climbing as a streak fills coral. A student closes their laptop. Daylight. | VO: **"And the day you want to quit — your ledger remembers why you started."** | Swell peaks, then softens. |
| 0:26–0:30 | CTA. Clean light frame. Wordmark: **StudyLedger**. Coral underline inks in beneath it — the same ledger rule from 0:08. Below: **studyledger.in** | VO: **"Start your ledger."** On-screen: **Start your ledger → studyledger.in** | Single resolved piano chord. Silence. |

### Script principles honored
- Question-led: three of five VO lines are questions
- One wink, mid-sweep, deadpan — never preachy
- Never overwhelming: 48 tools shown as rhythm, not inventory
- Dark→light arc = struggle→clarity; the coral ledger line is the connective visual motif
- The hook works for all four viewer states (everyone has started over)
